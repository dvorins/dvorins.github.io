Written in the summer of 2023, bred in stillness brings together the poetry of Emma DeLaRosa with music written by Dvorin. Performed by Nicholas Salerno's chamber ensemble in December of 2023, the piece featured the spoken word performance of Karen Klein. DeLaRosa's poetry deals with stillness, isolation, and stagnation in various stages of life. Dvorin's music serves as another player in the drama that unfolds, framing the scenes and providing an added depth to DeLaRosa's poetry.
