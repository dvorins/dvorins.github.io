Written in 2022 over the course of the entire year, each night i carry myself to you is a one act chamber opera in three scenes. The libretto, written by Dvorin and Isabella Putorti, deals with two characters. One is stuck in their ways, unable to have the empathy to see the other. The other, childlike, is upset and tried in vain to seek comfort from the other. Devoid of place, time, gender, the story deals with how grief and comfort can go wrong. The original performance featured Natalia D. Perera-Del Valle and Chujun Li, with Nicholas Salerno conducting his ensemble.
